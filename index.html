FAQ

Before you order

How does checkout work?

Add all items to your cart, then use the PayPal checkout button. PayPal processes one payment for the full order and collects the customer's payment and shipping details.

Do customers need a PayPal account?

PayPal usually allows eligible debit and credit card checkout depending on the customer's location and PayPal settings.

Can I request a custom piece?

Yes. Use the contact form and describe the style, color, and occasion. Karen can confirm availability and timing.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Keep pieces dry, store them separately, and avoid perfumes, lotions, and harsh cleaners. A soft cloth is best for gentle polishing.
