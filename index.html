Meet the maker

"I started making jewelry at my kitchen table, and I never stopped — because seeing someone light up over a piece I made is the best feeling in the world."

Hi! I'm Karen. I'm a self-taught jewelry maker with a deep love for polymer clay, resin, and finding beauty in small, everyday things.

Every earring, keychain, and bracelet I make is crafted by hand in my home studio. I put real care into every piece — from the colors I choose to the way it's packaged before it ships to you.

— Karen

Maker & Founder, Karen's Custom Creations

Behind the scenes

How every piece
comes to life

01
🎨

Design & Inspiration

I sketch ideas from nature, color palettes I love, and requests from customers. No two batches are ever identical.

02
🤲

Hand-Shape & Pour

Clay is conditioned and shaped entirely by hand. Resin pieces are carefully poured and set — sometimes with flowers, glitter, or pigments mixed in.

03
✨

Cure & Finish

Clay is oven-baked; resin is UV-cured and sanded smooth. Every piece is polished until it's just right.

04
💌

Pack with Love

Each order is wrapped in tissue, tucked into a branded box, and shipped with a handwritten thank-you note.
